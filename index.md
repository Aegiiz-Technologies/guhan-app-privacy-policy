**Introduction**

Guhan is an internal application used by authorized employers of Guhan to manage and follow up on financial clients. We respect user privacy and are committed to protecting any information handled within the app.

This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and safeguarded when using the Guhan application.

**Information We Collect**

The app may collect and store the following information strictly for management purposes:

Employer account details (name, phone number, email)

Client management data entered by employers

Follow-up notes and status information

**How We Use Information**

The collected information is used only to:

Manage and organize financial client records

Track follow-ups and internal workflows

Provide access only to authorized employers

⚠️ Guhan does not process payments or financial transactions.

**Data Sharing**

We do not sell, rent, or trade user or client data

Data is not shared with third parties for marketing or advertising

Information is accessed only by authorized personnel

**Third-Party Services**

Guhan does not use any third-party services, analytics tools, or external SDKs.

All data is handled internally and is used only for app functionality and authorized employer access.


**Data Security**

We implement appropriate security measures to protect stored data against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

Access to the app is restricted to authorized employers only.

**Data Retention**

Data is retained only as long as necessary for business and management purposes or as required by internal policies.

**Children’s Privacy**

Guhan is intended for business use only and is not designed for children under the age of 13.

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
